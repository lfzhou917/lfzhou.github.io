Multi-Robot Coordination

Research Statement

My long-term vision is to enable long-term deployments of robots performing tasks in real-world. Here, the robots have to deal with uncertain knowledge of the environment. The environment itself may be changing. Robots may fail or if they are operating in adversarial environments, they may get attacked. My goal is to devise algorithms to make the robots resilient to failure and be aware of the loss from uncertainty. To do that, the robots may have to communicate with each other to replan. I will specifically focus on strategies that are selective in who the robots communicate with and when to communicate.
